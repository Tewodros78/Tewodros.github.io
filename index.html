Ph.D. Student in Mathematics · Howard University

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Mathematics at Howard University with a strong interest in applying quantitative and computational methods to address public health challenges in under-resourced communities. My research focuses on mathematical and statistical models of infectious diseases—with a special emphasis on HIV drug resistance dynamics and treatment outcomes.
